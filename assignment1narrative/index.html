    Introduction to Metamorphosis

    Monarch Life Cycle

    The Monarch Life Cycle, also known as metamorphosis, can be broken
    down into a series of stages that show the development from caterpillar
    to butterfly. There are four stages to the monarch lifestyle; egg, larve,
    pupa, and adult. Each stage varies in length depending on what generation
    of butterfly is being studied. In general, the complete transformation
    takes roughly 30 days.
