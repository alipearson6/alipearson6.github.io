Welcome!

Welcome to Sun Bread Bakery! We believe in the art of homemade baked goods. That’s why we handcraft each pastry with high quality, natural, and locally- sourced ingredients. Specializing in artisan breads, muffins, pastries, and a range of coffee blends, we’re a home bakery catering to all occasions throughout Duluth.
